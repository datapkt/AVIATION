AERIAL SURVEY RAILWAYS AND METRO

DATAPKT Aviation offers excellent railway infrastructure. Various railway agencies have benefited from our project planning, construction monitoring, and infrastructure assessment. We use cutting-edge surveying drones with high-resolution sensors to conduct airborne surveys, inspections, and monitoring with centimetre-level accuracy. Our expert GIS specialists and drone pilots work nonstop to meet or surpass deadlines. For railway or highway projects, our exact Aerial Survey Data contains orthophotos, elevation models, terrain models, 3D models, thermographic data, and more.
Our complete Aerial Inspection service checks railway tracks, infrastructure, assets, bridges, and more. Regular aerial inspections help transportation companies monitor their most precious assets and prevent calamities. Our skilled pilots can quickly collect airborne data without disrupting traffic on crowded railroad tracks while adhering to rigorous railway safety standards.

Planning

Pre-Construction Survey:
Our advanced drones are capable of flying at high speeds. We have provided railways with aerial survey and monitoring services, allowing them to effectively assess possible sites for new railway tracks, station yards, and railway stations. The first stage is to conduct a thorough survey of the area to determine whether it is suitable for future construction. Our expert drone pilots, who exclusively use high-end surveying drones, can collect data in a fraction of the time and at a lesser cost than traditional methods. Following the collection of thorough data, our team of GIS professionals processes and develops a broad range of topographical maps and 3D models depicting terrain, utility lines, drainage slopes, and existing risks to assess the viability of the new project.

Services we provide following pre-construction survey

  • Detailed Project Reporting (DPR)
  • Topographical Survey
  • 3D Modelling
  • GIS Mapping for track length
  • DTM, DSM, and DEM Creation
  • Contour Mapping
  • GPR Survey
  • Aerial Photogrammetry and Videography
  • LiDAR Survey
  • Land Use Land Cover

Construction

Project progress monitoring
This stage is vital for project budget management. Railway and metro project managers can use our aerial construction monitoring solution to save total construction time. The less time invested, the lower the expense! However, to ensure that we do not compromise on quality and deliver on time, our great and professional drone pilots are constantly present. They compile aerial data covering all areas of railway plans while maintaining strict safety standards. Following that, our post-production team expertly processes the aerial project video, focusing on critical features such as unfinished overbridge pillars, half-completed construction sites, etc. We also include track detection and standard measurement in our railway inspections.

Inspection

Railway bridges and buildings
Aerial inspection is the best answer since it allows you to access regions that are difficult or dangerous for humans. Railway bridges, for example, require detailed and accurate assessment. We provide safe and cost-effective disclosure and study of structural details without putting workers in danger. Our GIS professionals create multiple 3D models and topographical maps to assure the structural stability of buildings and bridges.
