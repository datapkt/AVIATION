Pre-Construction Survey

Our advanced drones are capable of flying at high speeds. We have provided railways with aerial survey and monitoring services, allowing them to effectively assess possible sites for new railway tracks, station yards, and railway stations. The first stage is to conduct a thorough survey of the area to determine whether it is suitable for future construction. Our expert drone pilots, who exclusively use high-end surveying drones, can collect data in a fraction of the time and at a lesser cost than traditional methods. Following the collection of thorough data, our team of GIS professionals processes and develops a broad range of topographical maps and 3D models depicting terrain, utility lines, drainage slopes, and existing risks to assess the viability of the new project.

Detailed project report

DPR is the initial step in any railway project, laying the groundwork for its successful execution. A well-crafted DPR is essential for railway projects leveraging state-of-the-art technologies to ensure durability, cost-effectiveness, and sustainability. DATA PKT offers comprehensive project reports, serving as the cornerstone for the planning and implementation of various railway construction endeavors. Utilizing innovative technologies like drones for DPR is particularly crucial during the planning phase, enhancing precision, construction quality, and cost-efficiency. Here are some key services we provide in our railway project reports

Surveying and Mapping

Our use of high-resolution cameras and LiDAR sensors enables swift surveying of extensive areas, generating detailed topographic maps and 3D models. This data aids engineers and planners in understanding terrain intricacies, identifying potential obstacles such as power plants or archaeological sites, and meticulously planning railway routes.

Site Inspection

Drones conduct regular site inspections, monitoring progress, identifying safety hazards, and ensuring adherence to design specifications. Capturing high-definition imagery from various angles, drones provide invaluable visual documentation for project stakeholders.

Earthwork Monitoring

Drones fly over construction sites, tracking earthmoving activities, measuring stockpile volumes, and monitoring cut and fill operations. This data empowers project managers to optimize resource allocation, track progress accurately, and promptly address deviations from the project schedule.

Route Optimization

DATA PKT assists clients in selecting optimal alignments between designated railway points, efficiently connecting cities, populations, and industries. Through UAV-collected site information, we produce orthomosaic maps, digital elevation models, and contours, resolving conflicts with existing structures. By digitizing revenue maps and estimating land costs, we analyze construction expenditure alongside stockpile volumes, obstacles, and ground features, offering a cost-efficient approach to railway construction. Leveraging these capabilities, we compile detailed project reports, ensuring successful railway project execution.

Inspection

Railway bridges and buildings

Aerial inspection is the best answer since it allows you to access regions that are difficult or dangerous for humans. Railway bridges, for example, require detailed and accurate assessment. We provide safe and cost-effective disclosure and study of structural details without putting workers in danger. Our GIS professionals create multiple 3D models and topographical maps to assure the structural stability of buildings and bridges.

  • Aerial inspection allows for the detailed and accurate assessment of hard-to-reach or hazardous areas, such as railway bridges, without endangering workers.
  • By providing safe and efficient disclosure and study of structural details, aerial inspection reduces costs associated with traditional inspection methods and mitigates potential risks.
  • Our GIS professionals create multiple 3D models and topographical maps to ensure the structural stability of buildings and bridges, offering precise and reliable data for thorough analysis.
Railway track

Drone inspection is the recommended option for scheduled inspection and repair of high-traffic railway tracks. Our established drone pilots collect Drone Data swiftly while adhering to all railway safety rules. Our GIS team then reconstructs the high-resolution aerial pictures into a variety of topographical maps, including 3D models, 3D point clouds, 3D textured meshes, and more. Orthoimagery is the process of perfecting images obtained by track inspection, Thermal Surveys, structural inspection, and other methods. The data collected is used to measure the length of the track, detect potential hazards, and assist railways in avoiding catastrophic damage.

  • Our experienced drone pilots swiftly collect data while adhering to all railway safety regulations, ensuring minimal disruption to high-traffic railway tracks.
  • The GIS team transforms high-resolution aerial images into detailed topographical maps, including 3D models, 3D point clouds, and 3D textured meshes.
  • Orthoimagery and other inspection methods are used to measure track length, detect potential hazards, and help railways avoid catastrophic damage.
Monitor the vacant land around the railway track

DATAPKT uses drones equipped with high-resolution cameras to conduct aerial surveys and monitor vacant land surrounding railway tracks. Deploy drones to capture detailed imagery of the land adjacent to the railway corridor, covering both sides of the tracks. We analyze the drone footage to identify vacant parcels, unauthorized encroachments, or potential areas for development. We conduct regular drone patrols to monitor changes in land use, illegal activities, or encroachments along the railway right-of-way and generate comprehensive reports highlighting vacant land parcels, their boundaries, and any observed encroachments or developments for railway authorities' review and action. This proactive monitoring approach helps safeguard railway assets, prevent unauthorized land use, and facilitate strategic land management decisions.

  • Utilize drones equipped with high-resolution cameras to capture detailed imagery of land adjacent to railway tracks, identifying vacant parcels and unauthorized encroachments.

  • Generate detailed reports for railway authorities, highlighting vacant land parcels, boundaries, and any observed encroachments or developments, aiding in strategic land management.

Engineering scale Planning

We provide drone services, which have become increasingly valuable in the field of railway engineering for various aspects of planning, monitoring, and maintenance. Here’s how our drone data services are helpful in engineering scale planning in railways:

Surveying and Mapping

Our UAV technology, equipped with high-resolution cameras and LiDAR sensors, can quickly and accurately survey large stretches of railway lines. This data is used to create detailed maps and 3D models of the terrain, which are essential for planning new routes or upgrading existing ones.

Inspection and Maintenance

Our drones can inspect hard-to-reach areas such as bridges, tunnels, and elevated tracks without the need for costly and time-consuming manual inspections. High-resolution imagery and thermal sensors can detect structural issues, track alignment problems, and vegetation encroachment.

Monitoring Construction Progress

Our uav data enhance safety by reducing the need for personnel to work in hazardous environments. we can inspect areas affected by natural disasters like landslides or floods, providing critical data for risk assessment and mitigation.

Asset Management

Accurate and up-to-date data from UAVs helps in managing railway assets efficiently. This includes tracking the condition of tracks, stations, and other infrastructure, which supports proactive maintenance and reduces the likelihood of service disruptions.

Environmental Assessment

Environmental assessments are crucial for railway projects, especially when expanding networks through sensitive areas. We monitor environmental impact, track changes over time, and ensure compliance with environmental regulations.

Disaster Response

In the event of an accident or natural disaster, our drone service can quickly assess the damage and help in coordinating emergency response efforts. We also provide critical information for decision-making and facilitate rapid recovery operations.

Cost and Time Efficiency

Using UAVs significantly reduces the time and cost associated with traditional surveying and inspection methods. We cover large areas in a short time, and our data processing capabilities speed up the delivery of actionable insights.

Precision and Accuracy

Advanced sensors and GPS technology enable us to collect precise data with high accuracy. This is crucial for engineering applications where detailed and reliable data is necessary for making informed decisions.

Data Integration and Analysis

Our UAV data can be integrated with Geographic Information Systems (GIS) and Building Information Modeling (BIM) for comprehensive analysis and planning. This integration helps in visualizing spatial data and making data-driven decisions.

In summary, our UAV data service supports railway engineering scale planning by providing accurate, detailed, and real-time information that enhances the efficiency, safety, and cost-effectiveness of railway operations and infrastructure development.

DTM

DTM’s (Digital Terrain Model) are detected using aerial photography at low heights with a geometrically calibrated camera. This enables the drone to obtain all heights of a surveyed terrain.Generating Digital Terrain Models (DTM) for highways with drones provides detailed elevation data essential for accurate design and construction planning.

Contour Mapping

our dronne data enables precise contour mapping for railways, offering critical topographic insights for streamlined track design and construction. This technology enhances project planning and execution, ensuring optimal route alignment and efficient drainage management for railways.

Orthomosaic

In railway infrastructure, we utilize drones to create orthomosaic imagery, stitching together aerial photographs with photogrammetry software. This comprehensive visual data enhances railway construction by facilitating detailed planning, monitoring, and quality assurance, ensuring efficient and safe project implementation.

DSM

DSMs, crucial in railway planning, capture Earth's surface features, facilitating comprehensive spatial analysis. Their depiction of above-ground elements is pivotal for effective land-use planning and infrastructure development, ensuring sustainability along railway corridors and contributing to urban development initiatives.

Aerial Photogrammetry and Videography

Aerial photogrammetry and videography enhance railway projects with detailed imagery, improving planning, monitoring, and documentation processes, thus ensuring better decision-making and increased project efficiency.
